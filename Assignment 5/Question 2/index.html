Welcome to the City of Lights

Paris, the capital of France, is known for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Its picturesque 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine.

A Glimpse into History

Founded in the 3rd century BC, Paris has been a center of power, art, and revolution. From the French Revolution to the Belle Époque, every street tells a story.

Iconic Landmarks

Paris is home to world-famous landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum — each offering a unique glimpse into France’s rich heritage.

Delicious French Cuisine

From buttery croissants to gourmet dishes, French cuisine delights taste buds around the world. Parisian cafés are perfect for savoring every bite while watching the world go by.
